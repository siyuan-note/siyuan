* [改进数据库绑定块更改类型时的处理](https://github.com/siyuan-note/siyuan/issues/12152)
* [改进 sub/sup 作为纯文本粘贴](https://github.com/siyuan-note/siyuan/issues/12155)
* [发布服务禁止查找替换](https://github.com/siyuan-note/siyuan/issues/12168)
